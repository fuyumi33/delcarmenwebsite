Sugba Lagoon

Sugba Lagoon: A Paradise Unveiled

Sugba Lagoon is one of Siargao's top rated destinations. Almost completely enclosed in a small island off of Siargao, the water in the lagoon is this outrageous blue-green. From the air, Sugba Lagoon resembles a strange maze of vibrant water separated by small islands and domed mountains.

Sugba Lagoon beckons you with its irresistible charm, a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Imagine a haven of tranquility where crystal-clear waters embrace lush greenery, creating a breathtaking tapestry of nature's beauty.

Kayak across the lagoon's mirror-like surface, the gentle ripples whispering secrets carried on the breeze. Dive into the cool, refreshing depths, and let the worries of the world melt away as you surrender to the serenity of this enchanting paradise. Sugba Lagoon is more than just a lagoon; it's an experience that will leave you breathless and yearning to return.

Unleash Your Adventurous Spirit at Sugba Lagoonn

For the thrill-seeker within, Sugba Lagoon offers a playground of exciting water activities. Plunge into the invigorating waters and explore the vibrant underwater world teeming with life.

Glide across the lagoon on a paddleboard, the gentle rocking a symphony of relaxation.

Adrenaline junkies can test their limits with exhilarating water sports, while families can create lasting memories with fun-filled activities. Sugba Lagoon caters to every desire, ensuring an unforgettable adventure for all.

Boat Fee : 2,150

Environmental Fee : 50 per person
