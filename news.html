HAPPENING NOW: The new sea route is now open as of today, April 23rd at Del Carmen, Siargao Islands, Surigao del Norte. April 26, 2024

HAPPENING NOW: The new sea route is now open as of today, April 23rd at Del Carmen, Siargao Islands, Surigao del Norte. Local Government Unit of Del Carmen, headed by Mayor Jr Coro, extends a warm welcome to the Cagdianao municipal officials and employees for the official opening of the new sea route connecting Cagdianao, Dinagat Islands, and Del Carmen, Siargao Islands. Let's enhance connectivity and promote economic growth between our communities.

Virtual Meeting of Youth Health Clubs of Philippines and Nigeria March 26, 2024 | Del Carmen, Philippines | Nigeria April 26, 2024

GU Del Carmen and DentalCare Foundation based in Nigeria collaborated to establish Youth Health Clubs and allow both communities to pilot an engagement. The Selected Youth Club Members of Del Carmen introduced themselves, taught a few words related to the dental area, shared a presentation on tooth care and had a wonderful time. Its a wonderful thing to see children without biases trying to reach out and teach each other how to embrace the global culture. "ANG TANAN PARA SA TANAN."

MOA Signing with AGREA Foundation (AGREA) March 15, 2024 | Del Carmen Municipal Hall April 25, 2024

AGREA Foundation, is an innovative and inclusive organization that aims to help eradicate poverty for farming and fishing families, to alleviate the effects of climate change and to help establish food security in the Philippines.

Our long term partnership will focus on empowering women in addressing food security of the family, and developing technology based solutions for food security small island economies. We hope to be able to share Philippine Ingenuity and voice in transforming food systems at least for coastal communities around the world. "Ang Tanan para sa Tanan!"
