Tourist Spot

Sugba Lagoon

Sugba Lagoon is one of Siargao's top rated destinations. Almost completely enclosed in a small island off of Siargao, the water in the lagoon is this outrageous blue-green. From the air, Sugba Lagoon resembles a strange maze of vibrant water separated by small islands and domed mountains.


Pamomon Beach

Kawhagan Island and Pamomoan Beach are two incredible spots near Siargao Island and part of an island-hopping day-trip, which includes Sugba Lagoon. Kawhagan is a stand-alone island with a short sandbar, it’s incredible at low-tide. Pamomoan is a fantastic beach on Caob Island with a small restaurant, huts to relax in and, of course, hundreds of coconut palms.

Mariden Swimming Pool

Escape to paradise on Siargao Island at Mariden Resort, a stunning public pool resort nestled amidst the lush green mountains of Brgy. Lobogon. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of nature as you relax and unwind in our tranquil oasis. With its exceptional service and awe-inspiring views, Mariden Resort is the perfect getaway for your next vacation. Experience the best of Siargao Island's paradise at Mariden Resort. Book your stay now and indulge in a memorable vacation like no other, conveniently located near Siargao Airport (IAO).


Tabigue Aquatic Pool

Craving a refreshing escape this summer? Look no further than Tabigue Aquatic Pool Del Carmen, your one-stop destination for family fun in the heart of Del Carmen! Dive into cool, crystal-clear waters and beat the heat with exciting activities for all ages. Whether you're seeking a relaxing oasis or a place to create splashtastic memories with your loved ones, Tabigue Aquatic Pool Del Carmen offers the perfect blend of fun and unwinding. So come on down, make a splash, and experience an unforgettable summer adventure at Tabigue Aquatic Pool Del Carmen!


Quezon Lagoon

Unveil the beauty of Quezon Lagoon, a hidden gem cradled amidst nature's embrace. Embark on a captivating adventure, where serenity meets thrilling water activities. Kayak across the lagoon's glistening surface, marveling at the vibrant tapestry of flora and fauna. For the adventurous soul, plunge into cool, refreshing waters, perfect for swimming, diving, or simply unwinding on a relaxing paddleboard ride. Quezon Lagoon offers an unforgettable escape, promising a tranquil paradise for nature enthusiasts and adrenaline seekers alike. So, dive into a world of wonder and discover the magic of Quezon Lagoon!
